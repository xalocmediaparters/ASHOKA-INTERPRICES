MNRE Solar Subsidy

We help you avail the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE) subsidies for solar installations, making clean energy more affordable. The Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE) offers a subsidy program to make solar energy accessible and affordable for everyone. Through this government-backed initiative, homeowners can receive up to 40% financial assistance on rooftop solar installations. This means lower upfront costs, quicker return on investment, and long-term savings on electricity bills.
